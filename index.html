What is the triangle area?
The area of a triangle is the space that is present with in the boundries of a trianble.
How to calculate triangle area?
To calculate triangle area you should use the following formula:
A = (h * b) / 2
where:
b = base
h = height

The triangle area calculator is used in order to find the space enclosed by a trianble.
